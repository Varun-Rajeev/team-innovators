About Natural Disasters

Natural disasters are extreme, sudden events caused by environmental factors that can result in significant damage, loss of life, and disruption of communities.

  • Earthquakes: Sudden shaking of the ground due to tectonic shifts.
  • Floods: Overflow of water submerging land that is usually dry.
  • Hurricanes: Intense tropical storms with strong winds.
  • Wildfires: Uncontrolled fires in forests or grasslands.
